Courses Offered

Courses Offered

Explore our wide range of camping gear services.

  • Diploma in nursing Assistance

    Our two-year Diploma in Nursing Assistant (DNCA) program equips you with the fundamental skills and knowledge necessary to provide basic patient care in hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare settings. You will gain a strong foundation in nursing fundamentals, medical-surgical procedures, medication administration, patient assessment, and communication skills.

  • Diploma in home care Nursing

    The Diploma in Home Nursing (DHN) program is a comprehensive course designed to prepare you to provide quality care to patients in their homes. You will learn about essential nursing techniques, patient care services, first aid, wound management, disease management, medication administration, patient education, and effective communication skills.

  • Diploma in Hospital Nursing & Midwifery

    This program is designed to train students in assisting childbirth and nursing inside hospital premises. You will become comprehensive about basic mandatory nursing techniques, disease management, medication administration, patient education, and effective communication skills

  • Diploma in operation theater assistant

    This two-year program focuses on teaching the essential skills and knowledge required to function effectively as an operation theatre assistant. You will learn about equipment operation and management, aseptic techniques, patient care in the operating room, and assisting surgeons during surgical procedures.

  • Diploma in medical Laboratory Technology

    This two-year program provides in-depth training in medical laboratory procedures and techniques. You will gain expertise in performing various laboratory tests, analyzing samples, interpreting results, and contributing to disease diagnosis and treatment plans

  • Diploma in Patient care and First aid

    Our Diploma in Patient Care and First Aid program equips you with the skills and knowledge to provide basic first aid, manage medical emergencies, and administer essential patient care. You will learn about CPR, wound care, vital sign monitoring, and emergency procedures.

  • Diploma in medical Technician

    This program is designed to train students in the operation and maintenance of medical equipment used in various healthcare settings. You will gain a comprehensive understanding of medical equipment functionality, troubleshooting techniques, and patient care procedures.

  • Diploma in ICU Technician

    Our Diploma in ICU Technician program equips you with the skills and knowledge to thrive in the fast-paced environment of intensive care units (ICUs). This program prepares you for a dynamic and crucial role, directly supporting critically ill patients and assisting the ICU healthcare team.

About Us

Discover our story in providing Health and Education services.

Our Story

Experience the excellence of our services, where we have been offering high-quality Siddha products and good care of patients. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction ensures that every customers can rely on us for their health and medical products.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide an educational experience that not only imparts academic knowledge but also offers practical skills training, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for their future endeavors. We strive to nurture their overall development, equipping them with the tools necessary for success in their professional and personal lives.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish ourselves as a premier institution renowned for cultivating healthcare professionals who are not only proficient in their technical skills but also embody empathy and compassion in their practice. We are dedicated to shaping individuals who will make a significant impact on the well-being of communities, driven by a commitment to excellence and a deep understanding of human care.
